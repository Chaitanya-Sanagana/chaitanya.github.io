Track & Trace System (Pharma)

Business & Product Analysis | FDA-Regulated Systems

Back to Portfolio

Context

Pharmaceutical company needed a Track & Trace system to comply with DSCSA/MHRA regulations, ensuring traceability of parent-child packaging relationships.

Problem

  • Manual fragmented tracking of packaging hierarchies
  • High audit risk due to inconsistent data
  • Slow approval and validation cycles

Responsibilities

  • Stakeholder interviews with QA, Regulatory, IT, Operations
  • Translated requirements into SRS
  • Designed UML use-case and activity diagrams
  • Defined acceptance criteria & UAT scenarios

Actions

  • Authored 40+ functional requirements (FDA 21 CFR Part 11)
  • Created workflows mapping serialization and aggregation
  • Supported UAT and defect triage with JIRA & SQL queries

Outcome & Impact

  • Reduced development rework by 35%
  • Improved audit readiness and traceability
  • Faster regulatory inspections

Skills Demonstrated

Business Analysis, Requirements Gathering, UAT, Regulatory Compliance, SQL Validation
